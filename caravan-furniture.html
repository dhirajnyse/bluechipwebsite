Purpose-built interiors

A furniture system, not a loose collection

The layout, load plan, and operating cycle shape every component.

Accommodation and office caravans demand more from furniture than a conventional room. Units must use every millimetre, survive transport, support frequent cleaning, and remain serviceable through intensive occupancy.

We develop cabinetry, beds, desks, seating, and storage as an integrated package around the caravan GA drawing, service zones, openings, circulation, and required occupancy.

Substrate weight, hardware, edge protection, fixing points, and replacement access are resolved before production so the final installation is practical as well as compact.
