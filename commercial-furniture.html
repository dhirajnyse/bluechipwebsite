Commercial programmes

Built around people, traffic, and operations

Custom furniture that earns its place on the floor.

Commercial furniture has to resolve workflow, ergonomics, brand character, maintenance, and construction interfaces while remaining robust enough for repeated public or staff use.

From a single reception desk to a multi-floor rollout, digital production and sample approvals protect dimensional consistency, finish control, and programme certainty.

Performance by specification

Ergonomics, durability, and warranty aligned to the brief.

Working heights, reach zones, leg clearances, seating geometry, accessible counter positions, and cable routes are developed against the project’s stated ergonomic and accessibility requirements.

Construction, surface resistance, hardware duty rating, and replacement access are matched to expected occupancy. Project-specific warranty terms, exclusions, and maintenance obligations are defined transparently at quotation and contract stage.
