Enterprise Level Whistleblowing Platform Implementation & Support

ADNOC Distribution

Cyber Security, PCI-DSS Compliance & Certification

Requirements: PCI DSS Annual Certification.

Solution: Pre-assessment, scoping, awareness session, PCI DSS gap assessment, remediation support, wireless analyzer scanning, ASV vulnerability assessment, penetration test, PCI and PII data discovery scans.

Benefits: Prevents data breaches, helps comply with other standards, and increases business growth.

ADNOC Gas

Digital Twin

Requirements: An application to monitor the corrosion protection system for the pilot gas pipeline of 100 KM length.

Solution: Created a 2D GPS coordinated digital model of pipeline and integrated real time sensor data regarding the cathodic and external corrosion.

Benefits: We used the digital application to monitor and predict corrosion as well as alert concerned teams, thus enhancing the pipeline safety with reduced manual efforts.
