A controlled route to production

Design. Fabricate. Deliver.

Our process turns your layouts, quantities, specifications, and finish expectations into production-ready furniture packages.

  1. 01

    Design

    Review drawings, survey dimensions, resolve interfaces, select materials, and approve samples before release.

  2. 02

    Fabricate

    CNC route, edge-band, weld, upholster, assemble, and inspect against the approved production package.

  3. 03

    Deliver

    Pack by room or unit, coordinate phased delivery, and support installation, snag closure, and handover.
